Wa Gateway
WhatsApp Gateway, often referred to as a "WA Gateway," is a technology or service that enables businesses and developers to integrate their systems or applications with the WhatsApp messaging platform. It acts as a bridge between WhatsApp and external systems, allowing for the automation of messages, interactions, and notifications. A WhatsApp Gateway typically provides APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) that developers can use to send and receive messages to and from WhatsApp users. This is particularly useful for businesses that want to engage with their customers through WhatsApp, automate customer support, send transactional updates, and even build chatbots for interactive conversations.
